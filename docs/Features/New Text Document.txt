# Lead Management Module

## Purpose

Manage potential customers from initial enquiry until conversion.

---

## User Stories

- Add a new lead
- Edit lead information
- Delete a lead
- Search leads
- Filter leads
- View lead details
- Update lead status
- Assign lead to sales executive

---

## Lead Fields

- Full Name
- Company
- Phone
- Email
- Source
- Status
- Assigned To
- Address
- Notes
- Created Date

---

## Lead Status

- New
- Contacted
- Qualified
- Proposal Sent
- Negotiation
- Won
- Lost

---
